Spotify Dream Mode.

Brief:

Market Spotify's audiobooks to a young adults and teenagers.

Background:

90% of young adults use an electronic device during the hour before bed.

Problem:

While falling asleep to an audiobook seems like an ideal solution, it poses an issue. Books require continuity, so if listeners fall asleep while the book is playing, they will have to backtrack when they next listen.

Unexpected Insight:

Sleep apps already use the accelerometer in our phones to pinpoint the moment you we asleep.
