Gear IV Hell.

Brief:

Create a launch campaign for Diablo 4 that breaks through culture.

Background:

Diablo is set in an underworld inspired by Hell.

Unexpected Insight:

Hell is not a unique western concept. All over the world people have been gearing up for a journey after death; packing their coffins with spellbooks, maps, and coins, preparing for a fight through the underworld.

Solution

Encourage people to engage with their own culture and equip themselves with the same "hell gear" their ancestors used by visiting museums.
